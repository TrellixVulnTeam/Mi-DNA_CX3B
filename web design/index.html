Mi-DNA: an integrated sequence variant interpretation pipeline

This web application will help you understand, analyze, and store a wide variety of genetic information when provided with data. It was designed with ease of use in mind and freely accessible to everyone. The functionalities combine those of Varfish, Exomiser, MutationDistiller, Doc2HPO, Phen2Gene, and IEMBase. For more information, please navigate to the about page.

To continue your previous work session, please log in. If you are a new user, you can create a new account by navigating to the 'Register' site near the top right corner. Registering for an account will allow you to save your work and access your projects at a later time. If you do not wish to create an account right now, you can begin an analysis by navigating to the 'Import' page.
